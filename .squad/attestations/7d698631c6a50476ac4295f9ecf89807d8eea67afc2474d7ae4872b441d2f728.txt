## Suggestion filed as follow-up — CHORE-019

Reviewer flagged a window-selector race: rapid 24h → 7d → 30d
flips can let an older fetch resolve last and overwrite the
newer window's data. Fix is a render-token sentinel checked
post-await. Filed as CHORE-019 with reproduction notes,
suggested patch shape, and AC. Not blocking FEAT-056 — the race
recovers on the next user interaction and never produces stuck
charts.
